Welcome, Adventurer!


Step into the tavern and explore new adventures with fellow travellers. Feel free to rest, take stock of your party and prepare for your next quest over a tankard or two of my finest ale.

Select the Characters option from the menu to create or manage your rosters.

You can also team up with other adventurers with the Party tab and check their overall health or mana levels

We also cater for Dungeon Masters who can register straight away and keep a watch on the whole party during your quest via the Sign-up/Log-in menu tab.

To return to the bar, just click the BattleRoster Logo.

Any problems, or if that fella in the corner is bothering you, just let me know in the Contact tab.
